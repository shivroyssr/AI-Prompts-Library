ChatGPT Research Prompts
Purpose: This collection contains prompts for conducting structured research, summarizing information, comparing options, and generating actionable insights.

Prompt 1 – Topic Research
Objective: Understand a topic from beginner to advanced level.
Prompt: Act as a professional researcher.
Explain the topic below in simple language.
Topic: [Insert Topic]
Include:
- Definition
- Key concepts
- Benefits
- Challenges
- Real-world applications
- Latest trends
- Recommended resources
Present the information using headings and bullet points.

Prompt 2 – Company Research
Objective: Research a company before applying for a job.
Prompt: Research the following company.
Company: [Company Name]
Provide:
- Company overview
- Products or services
- Industry
- AI initiatives
- Recent developments
- Company culture
- Skills valued by recruiters
- Interview preparation tips

Prompt 3 – Market Research
Objective: Analyze a market or industry.
Prompt: Conduct a market research report for:
Industry: [Industry Name]
Include:
- Market size
- Growth trends
- Major competitors
- Customer segments
- Opportunities
- Risks
- Future outlook
